Security Services

CCTV Installation

We offer comprehensive and professional solutions to safeguard your premises and enhance security measures. With state-of-the-art technology, expert installation, and a commitment to customer satisfaction, we provide reliable and effective surveillance systems tailored to your specific needs

Intercom Installation

Providing efficient and reliable communication solutions that enhance security, convenience, and productivity within your premises. With cutting-edge intercom systems and professional installation expertise, we offer tailored solutions to meet the unique needs of residential, commercial, and industrial environments.

Alarm Installation

Our alarm systems utilize the latest advancements in detection technology to ensure early and accurate threat detection. From motion sensors and glass break detectors to smoke and heat detectors, our systems are equipped with cutting-edge sensors that can promptly identify potential risks and alert you or the authorities.

Project: Smart Transact Point of Sale

Our Mobile Point of Sale (mPOS) system empowers businesses to accept payments anytime and anywhere using a smartphone or tablet. With our secure and user-friendly solution, you can transform your mobile device into a portable cash register, providing a seamless checkout experience for your customers and enhancing your sales process.

Project: School ERP

Our School ERP is a comprehensive software solution designed to streamline and automate various administrative tasks within educational institutions. From managing student information to handling academic records, financial transactions, and communication, our School ERP simplifies school management, enabling educators to focus on what matters most – providing quality education.

Project : Site Progress Tracking

Our Site Progress Tracking System is a comprehensive solution designed to simplify and enhance construction project monitoring.
